Hi, I'm Ant. I'm a designer!

What I do

Brand concepts: Name your project, write a mission statement, identify core values, define positioning and tonality.

Visual identity: Translate your brand concept into a unique visual identity.

Design systems / pattern libraries: Holistic UI systems for digital products at any scale.

Grow and shape design teams: Define strategic design needs and hire the right people.

Mentoring for designers: Dedicated support for creative teams without middle management.

MVP product design: Ship your first product with lean and pragmatic UX process.

About me

I'm a maker: Finding the perfect balance between function and aesthetics has fascinated me ever since I was a kid. I love to build stuff that works and looks cool. I became a designer by building websites and I like to design in code.

I'm a generalist: I work across design displines and try to have a functional overview of adjacent displines. I think in systems and try to connect the dots.

I'm a practitioner: I'm a student of creative practice and process. I love working with creative people and applying principles of design and creativity across scales and contexts.
