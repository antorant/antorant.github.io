Hi, I'm Ant.

At Native Instruments I work on concepts and workflows for music creation hardware products.

With Wavepaths I helped the founding team refine and evolve their brand identity, and helped the product team ship version one.

At Idagio I helped to build and scale a music streaming service, then led product and brand design teams.

Since 2010 I’ve worked as an interdisciplinary product and brand designer.

About me

I'm a maker: Finding the perfect balance between function and aesthetics has fascinated me ever since I was a kid. I love to build stuff that works and looks cool. I studied art and began my career building websites: I'm happy working with paper, pixels or code.

I'm a generalist: I work across multiple design displines and have a functional understanding of adjacent displines. I think in systems and connect the dots.

I'm a practitioner: I'm a student of creative practice and process. I love working with creative people and I'm interested in applying principles of design and creativity across different scales and contexts.
