Design & Code

Hello, I’m Ant. I’m a digital product designer - everything from sketching out concepts and features to building interfaces (via words and pretty pictures).

I work in text documents, wireframes, mockups, prototypes, conversations, scribbles, HTML, CSS, JavaScript and silly gifs.

Currently I’m occupied by IDAGIO, designing and building the web side of our product.

It’s difficult to fit my daily work into what you’d expect from a designer’s portfolio; rough sketches and code don’t look very impressive out of context. I try to use my Dribbble profile as an improvised visual diary: the good, the bad and the ugly.

What else?

Both my visual style and my processes incorporate ideas of brutalism and minimalism. I believe in material honesty, consistency and reduction. I like to work logically and incrementally.

Code-based side projects are rare but usually involve random numbers and pretty colours.

I’m a lifelong musician and occasional DJ. I love playing and watching football, and I’m currently an enthusiastic student of the beautiful German language.

Thanks for stopping by. Give me a shout, if you like.
