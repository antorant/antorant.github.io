Dedicated mentoring for designers

Design is tricky. Our work is multidisciplinary and the landscape shifts constantly. We have to solve new and undefined problems, working with everyone from customers to founders, from marketers to engineers.

If you're starting out, it's not easy to find your feet among emerging disciplines and shifting role definitions. Everyone needs a designer, so you have the responsibility to choose the right projects and align your work with your values.

Most design roles come with personal development plans, but they can take a back seat to business goals. The people there to help you might have other priorities, or their ability to help you might be constrained by politics or hierarchy.

Working with an independent mentor can amplify your own growth efforts. With a focused feedback loop you'll learn and reflect faster, and with a committed partner you're more likely to hit your goals.

I can help you with:

  • Critique and feedback on your documents, presentations, wireframes and visual designs
  • Guidance on research, processes, running workshops and project management
  • Guidance on job applications, your portfolio, CV, online presence and approaching salary discussions
  • Written and verbal communication skills
  • Optimising your creativity and productivity
  • Troubleshooting interpersonal problems and cultivating healthy relationships with managers, stakeholders, engineers and other creatives
  • Overcoming imposter syndrome, managing stress and finding a healthy work-life balance

What you get:

  • One-to-one conversations at a cadence that suits you: monthly, twice monthly or weekly
  • Session notes for each conversation
  • Tools and resources relevant to your growth
  • I'll reply to questions by text or email within 24 hours
  • NDA-signed, total confidentiality

Who am I?

I'm Ant, I've worked as a designer for over a decade at startups, midsize companies and as a freelancer. I've been a designer, a team lead and a director, and the most satisfaction I found was in helping talented people to do their best work. I believe in the exponential power of learning, self-reflection and high-fidelity relationships, and that designers can change the world for the better at every scale.
