The approach

I provide a confidential 1:1 space for listening and reflection, for philosophical and practical perspective. Conversations of 60-90 minutes, either on the phone, video call or in-person in Berlin. It’s not professional psychotherapy: it’s intended as a complement to professional work, a more accessible alternative, or a guide to the right help.

My background and motivation

My work as a designer involves understanding people, guiding conversations and exploring problems. Alongside this work I study self-development, psychology and philosophy. I am taking part in educational seminars about psychoanalysis and Jungian psychology with a view to training as an analyst.
