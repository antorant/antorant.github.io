Counselling

Pay-what-you-want support for adults

Conversations of 60-90 minutes, either on the phone, video call, or in-person in Berlin.

About me

My work as a designer involves understanding perspectives, guiding conversations, exploring problems and supporting peers. I'm a lifelong student of self-development, psychology and philosophy, and I'm studying psychoanalysis and analytical psychology. My motivation is to support people while gaining experience alongside my studies.

Some topics and theories I'm interested in: Jungian/archetypal psychology, personality types, developmental psychology, stoicism, buddhist philosophy, object relations theory, comparative religion, symbolism, dream work, psychosomatics.

Safety

As I'm not yet a qualified therapist, we work with the following principles:

  • Full confidentiality
  • No judgement, and no assumptions
  • I'm there to listen and support, not to advise or fix
  • I'm committed to my own personal psychological work
  • We talk about our conversational dynamics and working relationship

Pay what you want

We have two or three sessions to see how we work together, then agree on a fee that fits to your financial situation.
