Counselling

I offer free counselling for adults, around the following topics:

  • Relationships, love, family and friends — needs, attachment, and communication.
  • Work: communication, relationships, conflict, professional development.
  • Creativity, self-expression, artistic practice: writing, art, music.
  • Self-discovery, purpose and vocation.

→ Write to me or schedule a call

The approach

I provide a confidential 1:1 space for listening and reflection, for philosophical and practical perspective. Conversations of 60-90 minutes, either on the phone, video call, or in-person in Berlin. You can share whatever you feel comfortable sharing. It’s not psychotherapy: it’s intended as a complement to professional work, a more accessible alternative, or a step towards the right help.

My background and motivation

My work as a designer involves understanding perspectives, guiding conversations, exploring problems and supporting peers. Alongside this I study self-development, psychology and philosophy. I am taking part in educational seminars about psychoanalysis and Jungian psychology with a view to training as an analyst. Friends have suggested I might make a good therapist, and I feel obliged to explore that in the world.
